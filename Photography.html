Selected Works







The images presented were created between 2018-2024. They were created with a Nikon d800, 35mm and 120mm film, and 4x5 Black and White film. I studied photography at Georgia State University. I learned both a respect for craftmanship and to appreciate imperfections. I operate with the understanding that photographs are the visualizations of ideas.
